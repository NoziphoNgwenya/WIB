Linking Law & Anthropology

WIB Consulting aims to achieve a balance of relationships between the South African legal system, the study of human behavior, and technology. Collaboration of these fields provides a specialized understanding of the virtual and physical realms on which we rely in the corporate environment, thus ensuring informed decisions are made regarding the solutions provided for your Caught between the and because we understand both worlds, we are able to advise on current issues and predict future issues.

We are able to identify:
  • The phenomenon of competing laws and beliefs,
  • Overcome the inability of individuals to articulate laws and beliefs,
  • Observe the divergence between stated norms and actual outcomes, and examine the actual process used in trouble cases.

We recognized that the law is not only a body of binding obligations kept in force by the specific mechanism of reciprocity and publicity inherent in the structure of society, but also a unique expression of cultural premises. This makes it vital to provide a non-distorted and holistic understanding of the judicial process of non-Western societies, including the legal reasoning used to arrive at decisions. In order to do so we implement ethnographic methods in our research.
